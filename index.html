Frequently Asked Questions

What is your Integrated Pest Management service?

+
  • 1. Pest inspection & Findings
  • 2. Action (Pest control treatment)
  • 3. Monitoring & Evaluation
  • 4. Ensuring customer Satisfaction

What are the types of treatments used for Pest control?

+
  • Cockroach: Gel & Chemical spray.
  • Bed bug: Spray & Powder treatments.
  • Ants: Granules, gel & spray treatments.
  • Rat: Bait station, glue trap, snap trap and rat poison.

Are you serving in Sharjah?

+

Yes, we provide our pest control services throughout Sharjah and surrounding areas.

After the pest control service, should I leave my apartment?

+

For most treatments, you can return to your property immediately after the service is completed. However, for certain intensive treatments, we may recommend staying out for a few hours. Our technicians will provide specific instructions based on the treatment.

Do you offer Cleaning services in Villa?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive cleaning services for villas, including post-pest control cleaning to ensure your space is safe and clean.

Is there any warranty on the service?

+

Yes, we provide a warranty on our pest control services. The duration and terms vary depending on the type of treatment. Please contact us for specific warranty details.

Is pest control safe during pregnancy?

+

We use eco-friendly and low-toxicity treatments that are generally safe. However, as a precaution, we recommend that pregnant women avoid direct exposure during and immediately after treatment. Please inform our technicians if anyone in your household is pregnant so we can take extra precautions.

Are your pest control treatments harmful to children and pets?

+

Our treatments are designed to be safe for children and pets when applied correctly and given appropriate drying time. We use products that are approved for residential use and provide specific instructions to ensure the safety of your family and pets.

Will the treatment leave a strong chemical smell?

+

Most of our treatments have minimal odor that dissipates quickly. We also offer odorless options for sensitive environments. If odor is a concern, please let us know in advance so we can select the most appropriate treatment.

How quickly will I see results after treatment?

+

Results vary depending on the pest and infestation level. For most pests, you'll notice a significant reduction within 24-48 hours. Complete elimination may take a few days to a couple of weeks, especially for pests with eggs that hatch after treatment.
